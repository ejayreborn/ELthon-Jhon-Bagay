About EJ Luxura

EJ Luxura is more than a name - it’s a philosophy.

The word Luxura draws from the Latin root lux, meaning “light.” Light is the foundation of both photography and design, and it symbolizes clarity, beauty, and refinement. Paired with my initials, EJ Luxura represents my commitment to bringing out the finest details in every project — whether it’s enhancing the natural glow of skin in high-end retouching or creating compelling designs that illuminate a brand’s identity.

Luxury in Every Pixel

Who I Am

I’m Elthon Jhon Bagay, a graphic designer and professional photo retoucher. My expertise spans high-end skin retouching, cosmetics, and fashion models for e-commerce, alongside product design for advertising materials such as flyers, menus, and logos.

My Approach

At EJ Luxura, I combine:

  • Attention to Detail → delivering flawless skin retouching that looks natural yet polished.
  • Creative Design Thinking → producing designs that balance beauty and function.
  • Luxury Standards → ensuring every piece feels elevated, timeless, and impactful.

This is what makes EJ Luxura not just a brand, but a personal signature — a reflection of both who I am and what I create.
